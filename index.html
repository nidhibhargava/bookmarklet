A Place You Can Feel At Home...

Mother's is an award winning neighborhood tavern and restaurant located in Historic Federal Hill, a hip, up-scale night life and dining destination in the heart of Baltimore City. We're open 7 days a week serving breakfast on the weekends. We take pride in our food - 90% is homemade.

Let us cater your next event!
We can cater your events in your home, at the venue of your choosing, or in either of our 2 Private party areas. The Private Upstairs...room can serve 30-100. There are 2 rooms with a full bar, pool table, big screen TVs and private bathrooms. Learn more!

The Back Alley... this area has 2 large rooms and can accommodate larger groups of 100-250. We have 2 private bars, private dinning areas, private entrance right next to parking garage and the back alley space features 16 hd tv's.

Close to everything!
Mother's Federal Hill Grille is a short distance from the Inner Harbor, M&T Bank Stadium (home of the Ravens), Oriole Park at Camden Yards, Ft. McHenry & the Visionary Arts Museum. Mothers Federal Hill Grille is a great spot to enjoy Baltimore's fun, upbeat night life and a great meal.

Delicious food, fantastic service, amazing drinks!
Full restaurant and bar serving full breakfast, lunch and dinner. Some of our award-winning specialties are: MD crab cakes, mouth-watering burgers, MD seafood and fresh fish with a Caribbean influence, colossal salads and over stuffed sandwiches. All served by an incredibly knowledgeable and friendly staff. Enjoy our great selection of micro brews and specialty drinks as well.

Tailgate with us!
We are the home of the Purple Patio... the best football tailgating spot in the city. Visit PurplePatio.com

Bring the kids!
We have a full kids menu and provide our tables with crayons to color with before your meal arrives.
